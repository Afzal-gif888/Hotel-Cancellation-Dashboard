 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing hotel booking and cancellation data to identify trends, patterns, and key factors affecting cancellations.  
An interactive Excel dashboard was created with a strong emphasis on **year-wise analysis of total bookings and cancellations** to support data-driven decision-making for hotel management.

---

 Objectives
- Analyze hotel booking and cancellation trends over multiple years
- Perform year-over-year (YoY) comparison of total bookings vs cancellations
- Identify major factors contributing to booking cancellations
- Present insights using a clean and interactive Excel dashboard

---

Year-wise Bookings & Cancellations Analysis
- Developed a **year-by-year comparison** of total bookings and cancellations
- Identified years with higher cancellation spikes
- Observed long-term demand fluctuations and seasonal patterns
- Helped understand cancellation behavior over time rather than isolated periods

---

 Key Insights
- City hotels have a higher cancellation rate compared to resort hotels
- Bookings with longer lead times are more likely to be cancelled
- Repeat guests show significantly lower cancellation rates
- Customers with special requests are less likely to cancel
- Year-wise trends reveal peak periods of high cancellations
